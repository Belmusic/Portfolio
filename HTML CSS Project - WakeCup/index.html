Early history of coffee

Coffee grown worldwide can trace its heritage back centuries to the ancient coffee forests on the Ethiopian plateau. There, legend says the goat herder Kaldi first discovered the potential of these beloved beans.

Coffee rituals around the world

Throughout history and for many substances, many rituals have evolved in which those substances are crucial elements, just like coffee. For many people, sharing coffee is a ritual that has become not only a basic gesture of hospitality, but also a celebration of being together.
